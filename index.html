About Shape

Shape is a concept that helps us define our idea of "Beauty"

As part of an exploration of the concept of "Beauty" as "Aesthetics", I started exploring the three simple shapes: circle, triangle, square. I related them with the natural space and human body, creating different dynamics of movement.

In my study of shape and space I was influenced by Kandinsky's theories on colours, shapes and music.

This Is Not My Home

Between 27th-30th October 2018 a devastating storm hit the North of Italy, leaving total destruction in its path; forests that stood for hundreds of years were left in tatters. I shot these photos after 5 months of being away from my hometown, little of what i remember of the place of my childhood remaining intact, fratments of what I used to know are delicately pieced together and expressed through these photos

I wasn't there. And now the place isn't there anymore either

Pain, beauty and hope is elegantly captured, one can only attribute it to grace.

Bodies Of Art

Everybody is beautiful in their own way, for their diversity and being unique and for the features of their personalities

I asked a few different women to pick a painting that they felt presented by, and I pictured them naked with the image of the painting projected on them: that way, the woman, the body becomes art itself.

_adventurediaryofagirl

On the 14th September 2016 I moved to Porto, Portugal, for a 6 month Erasmus experience.

It was hot and super sunny: I had been looking forward to that experience for ages and it was finally reality.

On my first day I was scared to death: I was in a country that I didn't know, so unsure of myself and not speaking a word of Portuguese. I was also completely alone, not knowing anyone at all there. I spent my day in my room, crying and looking at the light.

This project started from there: everyday I took at least one picture of my life, of details which I saw and I thought were beautiful or significant, and posted it on my social media page which I created fro the purpose.

In the end, it was nothing at all what I had expected: it was a life changing experience, the best I have ever done in my life. I experienced love and hate, happiness and sadness, home-sickness and home-feeling, I grew and built a new family which will be in my heart forever. I learned to appreciate other cultures and that everyone has something interesting to say about themselves.If I was crying on my first day, I was out with new friends on my second. Most of all I learned to love myself, I learned my value as a person which was so diminished my the people from my hometown.

Bio

I was born in a small town in the north east part of Italy. Since I was about 16 I started doing photography, and my dad got me my very first camera. After high school I studied photography and started seeing the world. The world is beautiful and the more I see of it, the more inspired I get. You don't need to go far to see things: I take my car, drive around and create. Now I live in London. Determination and passion are the things that make a difference in our lives.
